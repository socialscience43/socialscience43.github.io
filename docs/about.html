About Social Science 43

“Philosophy is the gateway to innovation.”

Social Science 43

Welcome to Social Science 43! This is an independent, student-run, digital publication established in September 2025 to discuss economic and philosophical topics. The goal here is to create a community that explores introspective, profound topics about the world around us.

As the founder of SS43, I coded and designed the site on GitHub Pages. Please leave a comment on our posts if you like them, have any suggestions, or want to have a respectful debate. Thank you so much for checking us out, and have a wonderful day!
